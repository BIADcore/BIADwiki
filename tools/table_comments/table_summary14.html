Various biological markers of the health and biological markers of human remains. Each individual may have multiple markers, therefore IndividualID may occur in multiple rows.
Column name Data type Column description

Status varchar Status of the trait.
Position varchar

trauma_cause varchar Specific cause of skeletal trauma
trauma_direction varchar Direction of skeletal trauma, e.g. arrow to head or a backstab
