zoptions_MaterialCulture_MorphologySection

MorphologySection

Basic description of the object’s morphology when observed in section e.g., two buttons could have a circular morphology when looked at from the top, but one could actually form a half-sphere and the other a cone when looked at from the side. Morphology would be ‘circular’ in both cases, but Morphology (section) would be ‘half-sphere’ in one case and ‘conical’ in the other. In the case of a blade, morphology in section could be ‘oval’, ‘diamond-shaped’, etc.
