StrontiumEnvironment
Some publications provide locally relevant estimates of the baseline environmental strontium isotope ratios. SiteIDs need not be unique, therefore more multiple environmental baseline estimates can be recorded for a single site.

SiteID varchar
Soil varchar The environmental source. NULL and no are equivalent.

GroundWater varchar The environmental source. NULL and no are equivalent.

dentine varchar The environmental source. NULL and no are equivalent.
ice core varchar The environmental source. NULL and no are equivalent.
