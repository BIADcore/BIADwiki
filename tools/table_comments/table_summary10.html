FaunalSpecies
Records of all species identified at the phase level for each site, with their associated age and sex trends, pathologies, and body part representation.
Column name Data type Column description
FaunalSpeciesID varchar Unique identification number for each species record
PhaseID varchar Please see the detailed description of the 'Phases' table.
TaxonCode varchar Unique code to identify the full taxonomic name. This usually consists of the first four letters of the genus and first three letters of the species.
NISP int Number of Identified Specimens
SpeciesNote varchar General notes field for additional information about the species
AgeCriteriaBF enum Binary distinction between species that have been aged based on epiphyseal fusion (1) and those that have not (0)
AgeCriteriaDA enum Binary distinction between species that have been aged based on dental abrasion (1) and those that have not (0)
AgeCriteriaDE enum Binary distinction between species that have been aged based on dental eruption (1) and those that have not (0)
AgeCriteriaNR enum Binary distinction between species that have no age trend evidence noted (1) and those that do (0)
AgeNote varchar Age trend evidence notes
AgeTrend enum Description of the age trend e.g. 'mostly juvenile', 'mostly adult'
HorncoreNote varchar Horncore shape notes
HorncoreShape enum Interpretation of wild/domestic status based on the horncore shape e.g. 'Both wild and domestic', 'majority wild' etc.
Pathology enum Binary distinction between species that show evidence of pathology (1) and those that do not (0)
PathologyNote varchar Pathology evidence notes
PresenceOfFoetals enum Binary distinction between species that include foetal remains (1) and those that do not (0)
PresenceOnly enum Binary distinction between species that have been recorded as presence only (1) and those that have not (0)
SexCriteriaME enum Binary distinction between species that have been sexed based on metric criteria (1) and those that have not (0)
SexCriteriaMO enum Binary distinction between species that have been sexed based on morphology (1) and those that have not (0)
SexCriteriaNR enum Binary distinction between species that have had no sex trend evidence noted (1) and those that do (0)
SexNote varchar Sex trend evidence notes
SexTrend enum Description of the sex trend e.g. 'majority male, 'majority female' etc.
CitationID varchar
